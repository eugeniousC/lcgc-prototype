Default percentages from a standard residential construction draw schedule used by lenders for construction-to-permanent loans. Budget is what you plan to spend on each phase — adjust either the % or the dollars. Amount Paid is what you've actually paid out — type invoice totals as they come in. Overage % shows where you're over or under budget on each line. The progress bar reflects total paid against the full Total Project Budget (Build + O&P). You can also enter the Total Project Budget first and the planner reverse-calculates the Build Budget at the current O&P (cost-plus model). This tool is for planning estimates and job tracking — not a binding quote, contract, or loan disclosure.
