How We Work Together

Five steps from "what if"
to handing you the keys.

Building a home is the biggest project most families ever undertake. Our process is built to keep you informed, in control, and never wondering what happens next.

1
Step One
Discovery
A no-pressure conversation. We come to your home, walk the property, listen to what you actually want, and answer every question.
2
Step Two
Design
We sketch, model, and refine the plan with you. Floor plans, finish selections, structural calls — all locked in before the first nail.
3
Step Three
Estimate
A written, itemized proposal. Materials, labor, fees, timeline, contingencies — no surprises, no hidden line items.
4
Step Four
Build
Our crew, our subs, our standards. Weekly walkthroughs, photo updates, change orders documented in writing before any work begins.
5
Step Five
Handoff
Final walkthrough, punch list, manuals, warranties. We don't disappear after the check clears — we stand behind every home we build.
